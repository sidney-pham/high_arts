A Typical Aussie Summer

Sun Dec 13 2015 11:00:00 GMT+1100 (AEDT) William Winter

Feet scorch on the concrete
Blisters, Bindies burn
Santa in a singlet
Christmas bugs return

Air-Conditioning always powered
Crickets play their song
Lying alone in the dying heat
Awake, all night long

The beach always seems filled
The water crystal clear
Old men in their speedos
Let’s Crack open a Beer

Mozzies leave their red marks
Somewhere obviously seen
Bugs are flying everywhere
Someone get the Mortein

Maccas runs for Thickshakes
Bunnings BBQ
Zooper Doopers leave their scars
And thongs are worn, no shoes

Water fights with the hoses
Water Balloons are filled
Cold showers, Ice Bath
Burgers always grilled

We’ve never seen the snow
But we’re happy with the sun
We’ve grown to love the burning heat
Cause we’re the lucky ones
